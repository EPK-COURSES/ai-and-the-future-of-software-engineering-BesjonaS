# Topic: AI and the Future of Software Engineering

## Introduction

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is one of the most talked topics nowadays, especially related to Software Engineering.
People are curious to know how AI affects Engineers, because some of them are afraid they will be replaced. On the 
other hand some people see it a game changer. As a Software Engineering student myself, to have a more detailed and 
correct opinion on this, I am doing a research. The goal of this research is to know the current situation (adoption 
rates, other developer opinios, investments etc.) and to "predict" how it can affect the future in this field.
